üç≤ Slow Cooker Recipes

Mexican Carnitas

Ingredients: 3-4 lbs pork shoulder (cubed), 2 bell peppers (sliced), 1 onion (sliced), 4 cloves garlic (minced), 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp chili powder, 1 tsp oregano, 1 tsp smoked paprika, salt/pepper, 1 can diced tomatoes, 1/2 cup bone broth

Instructions: Cook on low 6-8 hours. Shred pork, serve over cauliflower rice or lettuce wraps. Top with avocado, cheese, and cilantro.

Beef & Mushroom Stew

Ingredients: 2-3 lbs beef chuck (cubed), 1 lb mixed mushrooms (quartered), 2 carrots (chunked), 2 celery stalks (chopped), 1 onion (diced), 4 cloves garlic (minced), 2 cups beef bone broth, 2 tbsp tomato paste, 2 tsp thyme, 1 tsp rosemary, 2 bay leaves, salt/pepper

Instructions: Cook on low 7-8 hours. Thicken with coconut flour slurry if desired.

Chicken Cacciatore

Ingredients: 6-8 chicken thighs (bone-in, skin-on), 1 bell pepper (sliced), 1 onion (sliced), 8 oz mushrooms (sliced), 1 can crushed tomatoes, 1/4 cup bone broth, 3 cloves garlic (minced), 2 tsp Italian seasoning, 1 tsp oregano, salt/pepper, fresh basil

Instructions: Cook on low 6 hours. Serve over zucchini noodles or spaghetti squash.
